Transportation Museum

Jawa Timur Park (JTP) in Batu City, Malang, consists of three parts: Jatim Park 1, 2, and 3, each with a unique experience. Jatim Park 1 offers amusement rides, Jatim Park 2 focuses more on education, and Jatim Park 3 has dinosaur replica attractions.

East Java Park

Museum Angkut features a collection of classic cars and President Soekarno's official helicopter, and organizes exciting shows every day. Visitors can also explore the museum's various thematic zones and enjoy culinary delights at the Nusantara Floating Market.

Village of Color

Village of Color is the result of an initiative by students of Muhammadiyah University of Malang since 2016, transforming a slum on the Berantas River into a charming tourist village. From revitalizing houses with colorful paint to developing local handicrafts.

Transportation Museum

The Angkut Museum in Batu City, East Java, is one of the favorite tourist destinations with a diverse collection of vehicles, including classic cars and President Soekarno's official helicopter, and offers various interesting shows such as Man On Fire, Vehicle Live Action, and Cars and Costume Parade everyday. Apart from that, this museum presents a unique experience with thematic zones such as Hollywood, Gangster, Chinatown, Batavia and Europe, as well as providing complete public facilities for visitors.

East Java Park

Jatim Park in Malang, East Java, is a leading tourist destination that offers exciting experiences for visitors of all ages. With three main complexes - Jatim Park 1, Jatim Park 2 (Batu Secret Zoo), and Jatim Park 3 (Dino Park) - each spot displays a variety of fascinating recreational rides and educational attractions. From a modern playground, interactive zoo, to rides to learn about ancient life, Jatim Park promises an unforgettable holiday experience for the whole family.

Village of Color

Village of Color in Malang is a unique tourist destination that offers a photogenic experience with colorfully painted houses, making it a favorite spot for visitors to capture moments with family or partners. Previously rundown, this village has now been transformed into a beautiful place thanks to the efforts of students at the University of Muhammadiyah Malang in 2016, with support from a paint company.

House of Sampoerna

House of Sampoerna in Surabaya, an Indonesian kretek museum, offers a rich experience of the history of Indonesian kretek as well as various interesting activities such as exploring the Sampoerna cigarette exhibition, touring the city of Surabaya for free with the Surabaya Heritage Track, enjoying coffee in a comfortable cafe, shopping for souvenirs, and adding photo collection with interesting spots in this museum.

Mosaic Park

Taman Mosaik, a new development project that has completed its first phase by the Surabaya City Government, offers a unique recreational experience with attractive colorful nuances. The location was originally an abandoned swamp, which has now been turned into a beautiful Instagrammable park. Built in early 2019, this 5,100 square meter park is an ideal place to take photos and relax with the family.

Sengkaling Park

UMM Sengkaling Recreation Park, owned by the University of Muhammadiyah Malang, offers a fun family recreation experience with various rides. Located on Jl. Raya Mulyoagung No. 188, Dau District, Malang Regency, this park is easily accessible from the center of Malang City and Batu City. With a land area of 9 hectares, including 6 hectares of gardens and trees, as well as water facilities from natural mountain sources, this place is a favorite choice for visitors who want to relax and enjoy a holiday with their family.

Waterland

Waterland Mojokerto, which is located on Jalan By Pass Mojokerto, close to the Mojokerto gas station, is a recreational destination that offers a variety of water entertainment rides and other facilities. Visitors can enjoy the flying fox, water park, racer slider, special children's pool, ATVs, and much more. With affordable ticket prices, namely IDR 25 thousand per person on weekdays and IDR 30 thousand per person on weekends, Waterland Mojokerto is an attractive choice for spending time with family or friends.

Pacet Mini Park

Pacet Mini Park, a family tourist destination in Mojokerto, East Java, offers an unforgettable holiday experience with a variety of exciting and affordable entertainment rides. Known as an icon of the city of Pacet Mojokerto, this place offers various rides suitable for all ages, from a water park with a large swimming pool to an exciting flying fox. Visitors can also enjoy ATV and fish therapy to relax themselves after playing on the rides.

Raden Sekar Park

Raden Sekar Park, located in Madiun, East Java, is a tourist forest that offers natural beauty with a stunning flower garden in the middle. Visitors can enjoy the calm natural atmosphere and interesting activities such as honey bee education and porang development. This place also provides Instagrammable photo spots and facilities such as a camping ground, making it an ideal destination to relax and enjoy the beauty of nature with the family.

Pine Tree House

Rumah Pohon Hutan Pinus Songgon, destinasi rekreasi alam di Banyuwangi, menawarkan ketenangan dan kedamaian dari hiruk pikuk perkotaan. Dikelilingi oleh hamparan pohon pinus yang rapi dan aroma getah pinus yang khas, tempat ini menjadi pilihan ideal bagi para petualang dan pencinta alam. Fasilitas seperti gazebo, ayunan, dan musholla tersedia dengan biaya masuk yang terjangkau. Pohon pakis yang tumbuh di sekitar area memberikan kesan alami, dan pengunjung bahkan dapat memetiknya untuk dijadikan kuliner sayur pelengkap hidangan.
