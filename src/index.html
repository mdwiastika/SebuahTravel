EAST JAVA

Flora & Fauna

East Java captivates with its diverse flora and fauna; from the exotic forests of Mount Bromo to long-tailed macaques and wandering deer in Baluran National Park, creating a charming and biodiversity-rich environment.

Tradition

The traditions of East Java enchant with majestic traditional dances like Reog Ponorogo, classical gamelan music, and local celebrations reflecting cultural diversity, bringing to life the province's ancient heritage and cultural beauty.

Geography

East Java, situated in the eastern part of Java Island, captivates with its diverse geographical landscape, from majestic mountains in Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park to exotic beaches like Malang Beach.

Banyuwangi, Jawa Timur
Blue Flame
At Kawah Ijen, experience the mesmerizing "Blue Flame" phenomenon, where spontaneously ignited sulfur gas creates a captivating blue-hued flame. The stunning natural landscape, featuring a turquoise-colored crater lake, adds to the allure of this extraordinary journey, providing insights into the splendor and uniqueness of this volcanic crater.
Gresik, Jawa Timur
Limestone Hall
In Gresik, discover a captivating limestone hill with unique formations surrounded by lush greenery. This tranquil spot not only showcases natural beauty but also hints at intriguing historical traces within the limestone formations. A brief journey to Gresik's limestone hill promises both serenity and the marvels of nature.
Probolinggo, Jawa Timur
Mount Bromo
Mount Bromo in East Java, Indonesia, is renowned for its stunning landscapes and captivating sunrise. The volcano's sandy crater provides a unique experience, while cultural significance is seen in Tenggerese ceremonies. Bromo offers a brief yet immersive blend of nature and culture.
Probolinggo, Jawa Timur
Ranu Kumbolo
Ranu Kumbolo: Tranquil alpine lake in Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park. Ideal for Mount Semeru trekkers, offering serene camping by crystal-clear waters. Embrace the cool mountain air, breathtaking views, and nature trails for a rejuvenating escape.
Malang, Jawa Timur
Ungapan Beach
Ungapan Beach in Malang boasts soft white sand, clear waters, and stunning cliffs. Visitors can enjoy the serene atmosphere, listen to the soothing waves, and partake in activities like snorkeling. It's an ideal destination for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty.

The Wonderful of East Java

Visit soon and savor an unforgettable holiday experience in East Java!

Rujak Cingur

Rujak Cingur, a traditional dish from East Java, is a unique and flavorful salad featuring a mix of fresh vegetables, cow's snout (cingur), and shrimp paste. The dish is renowned for its harmonious blend of sweet, savory, and spicy flavors, creating a delightful culinary experience that represents the rich culinary heritage of East Java.

Reog Ponorogo

Reog Ponorogo dance, typical of East Java, depicts courage and grandeur through masculine movements adorned with stunning costumes and traditional attributes such as the singo barong mask and accompaniment of gamelan music. Celebrating Ponorogo's cultural heritage, the dance often showcases the agility of the performers and exudes powerful energy, creating a magnificent and profound experience.
