Most popular Food & Snack Products

  • Rawon

    Java

    4,5

    Rawon is a traditional Indonesian dish originating from East Java. Its distinctive dark color broth is derived from the use of "kluwek" nuts and various spices. Typically made with beef, simmered until tender. It is served with steamed rice, boiled eggs, bean sprouts, and crackers

  • Rujak Cingur

    Java

    4,4

    Rujak Cingur, with its comprehensive and unique flavors, is one of East Java's unmissable culinary specialties when exploring cities like Surabaya. Featuring fresh tropical fruits, pieces of braised cingur, and fried tofu and tempeh, this salad creates a perfect harmony of flavors and textures.

  • Lontong Balap

    Java

    4,6

    Lontong balap is a traditional Indonesian dish originating from Surabaya, East Java. It consists of rice cakes ("lontong") served with bean sprouts, tofu, lentho (fried mung bean fritters), and fried shallots, all topped with a savory peanut sauce and sweet soy sauce. It's a flavorful and hearty dish often enjoyed as a popular street food.

  • Pecel Semanggi

    Java

    4,3

    Pecel is a traditional Indonesian salad featuring mixed vegetables like spinach and bean sprouts, accompanied by a robust peanut sauce made with ground peanuts, garlic, chili, tamarind, and palm sugar. It's often served with rice or lontong and may include fried tempeh or tofu as toppings

  • Nasi Bebek

    Java

    4,8

    Duck rice is an Indonesian dish consisting of rice cooked with special spices, served with crispy fried duck pieces that are crunchy on the outside and tender on the inside. It's typically accompanied by vegetables, sambal, and pickles. Its savory and delicious flavor makes it a popular choice for special occasions.

  • Tahu Tek

    Java

    4,4

    Tahu Tek is an Indonesian street food dish originating from Surabaya, East Java. It features fried tofu served with a flavorful peanut sauce, sliced cucumbers, beansprouts, and rice cake cubes. The dish is topped with crispy shallots and sometimes boiled eggs. Tahu Tek offers a delightful combination of textures and flavors
